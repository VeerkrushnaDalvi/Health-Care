Symptoms of a brain tumor can vary depending on the location and size of the tumor, but may include headaches, seizures, vision or hearing changes, balance problems, and changes in mood or behavior.

Diagnosis of a brain tumor typically involves imaging tests such as an MRI or CT scan, and a biopsy may be needed to determine the type of tumor.

Treatment for a brain tumor depends on the type, location, and size of the tumor. Options may include surgery, radiation therapy, chemotherapy, or a combination of these treatments.
